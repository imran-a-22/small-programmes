Chem Reaction Rush

Catch the correct product before it falls. Learn simple reactions through quick play.

Score: 0 | Lives: 3

Reaction:

Pick the correct product, then move the lab basket under it with arrow keys.

Learning notes

  • Word equations show what substances react and what new substance forms.
  • Example: acid + base → salt + water.
  • This game uses simple classroom-level patterns, not advanced balancing.
